FAQ

Q. What is Town Quiz?

A. Town Quiz is an online trivia quiz built around your local community. The quiz is advertised on social media and provides a new and exciting platform for member advertising, while promoting your community and local tourism.


Q. What do the ads cost our members?

A. The pricing will be established by your chamber.


Q. Who performs the sales of the ads?

A. The chamber will perform its' own sales by mail, phone or in person.


Q. Are the ads sold on an annual basis?

A. Yes, we request you sell the ads on an annual basis to keep costs down.


Q. Will our chamber receive any annual renewal income?

A. Yes, your chamber will receive the exact same percentage year after year even if the member renews their ad directly with WinGroups media through no effort on the part of your Chamber.


Q. What are our costs to get started?

A. Nothing. You establish your own prices and forward WinGroups Media your advertisers' information along with the payment to WinGroups Media.


Q. Is the quiz kept "fresh"?

A. YES! The quiz is updated periodically.


Q. What will be the number of page-views?

A. Generally, we try to obtain annual minimum page views of 300,000.


Q. What if a member contacts WinGroups directly to purchase an ad?

A. Your chamber still receives the same income as if they sold it themselves.


Q. Why a trivia quiz?

A. Quizzes are a fun, interactive form of digital media advertising that doesn't feel like traditional marketing. A normal digital ad is seen for a fraction of a second whereas our participants spend an average of 3.7 minutes playing the quiz.


Q. Will we be able to track the success of our member's advertising?

A. Yes. Each member will be sent annual statistics.


Q. Where can we see an example of the quiz "live"?

A. Visit townquiz.com to see the trivia quiz in action.


Q. Is there a limit to the number of ads we can sell?

A. No. There is no limit. We can work with one single ad or hundreds.
